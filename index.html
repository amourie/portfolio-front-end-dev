Stuff I ❤

Tropical birds, art deco objects, vintage jewellery, decor and foreign language films. Walks on the beach or by the river. Sunday markets stalls, pastries, croissants and great coffee. These are a few of my favourite things.

Inspirational things ⛅

Through-out my life I have been inspired by ambitious and hard-working people. I appreciate great design and fabulous aesthetics. I explore, am curious and adventurous. I value family and hardworking ethic as well as kindness.

The place I call home 🏠

Brisbane, Australia. The place I call home. If you've never been to Brisbane, then I invite you for a visit to this exceptionally beautiful sub-tropical river-city. She is a beauty to behold.
